About Me

G'day! I'm Samuel, born and raised in the vibrant city of Melbourne, Australia. My passions are as diverse as they are exciting: from the strategic depths of chess to the physical discipline of boxing, the cultural richness of learning Japanese to the thrill of AFL, and of course, the endless possibilities of coding. Each of these hobbies has shaped me into who I am today, always eager to learn and take on new challenges.

My Journey

Although I'm still in school, my life's journey has already been filled with incredible experiences. I've had the privilege of traveling to different parts of the world, each trip leaving an indelible mark on me. From carving through powdery slopes while skiing in Japan to savoring the most exquisite hot chocolate in a charming Italian hotel, these moments have broadened my horizons and fueled my curiosity about the world. As I continue to grow and learn, I'm excited about the adventures that lie ahead and the person I'm becoming.
